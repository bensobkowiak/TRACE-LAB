Transmission, Resistance, and Computational Epidemiology (TRACE) Lab

Welcome to the TRACE lab, based in the Department of Infection, Immunity and Inflammation at University College London.

Our research harnesses genomic analysis and bioinformatics to understand the evolution and transmission of infectious diseases. We integrate whole-genome sequencing, transcriptomics, phylogenetics, and mathematical modelling to explore antimicrobial resistance, pathogen diversity, and outbreak dynamics. Our research bridges laboratory science and public health, aiming to inform surveillance strategies and improve real-world response to microbial threats.


We welcome new postdocs and PhD students, if you are interested in joining, please get in touch.
